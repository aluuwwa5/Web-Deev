Where to go in Almaty?

The main sights of Almaty that you definitely need to visit:

  • Classic locations where you can go with the whole family: the Botanical Garden, the Park named after 28 Panfilov Guards, the Green Bazaar, the zoo, the aquarium, the water park. Go to the New Square to admire the beauty of the local architecture. It is also worth visiting the Old Square named after Abai and the Almaty Arbat – something interesting is always happening here.
  • Active recreation: Kok-Tobe, Medeu, Shymbulak are ideal places for active tourism, especially if you do not want to go far from the city.
  • City entertainment: cinemas, shopping malls. Choose any one – it will be very pompous, modern and beautiful everywhere. Hundreds of different cafes, restaurants, clubs, billiard rooms and other entertainment venues. At any time of the year, this city will welcome you with open arms – there is no bad weather here.

    • Almaty is literally created for tourism, so try to devote as much of your time to it as possible. Believe me, it's worth it!
